Abstract. The article discusses creation of an electronic textbook for educational institutions specialized in textile 

industry on the basis of artifact materials. The content of the textbook includes photographs of cotton fabrics samples 
dated from the 18th to 19th centuries, as well as sample-based vector clip arts, made in graphic editors. 

 
Keywords: multimedia, vector clipart, museum sample, chintz.
