THE GLOBALIZATION OF POPULATION AS A DISPLAY
OF RECURRENCE OF DEMOGRAPHIC PROCESSES

N.I. Ovechkina
Novosibirsk State University of Economics and Management

E-mail: daeq31@rambler.ru
The article is devoted to the consideration of the demographic globalization phenomenon.

The definition of the given concept is made, development periods are described. Much attention
is given to L. Gumilev’s passionarity and ageing of ethnos theory. Stages of globalization of
population are considered as stages of a life cycle of ethnos. From this point of view the
globalization of population is represented as one of forms of recurrence of demographic
processes.

Key words: recurrence, periodization, globalization, ethnos, the passionarity theory, race,
life cycle stages, postbiological community, tolerance, demographic processes.
