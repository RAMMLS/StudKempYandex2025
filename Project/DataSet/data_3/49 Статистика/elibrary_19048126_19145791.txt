INTEGRATED APPROACH TO THE QUALITY EVALUATION
OF THE SAMPLING OBSERVATION

E.S. Shmarikhina
Novosibirsk State University of economics and management

E-mail: stat2004@inbox.ru
The article considers an integrated approach to the quality evaluation of the sampling

observation – the term «statistical information actuality» is defined, classes and types of errors
as well as their sources at stages of a sampling observation are identified, the mechanism of
calculation of the «price» of error is suggested. The notion of the efficiency of sampling
observation is discussed, approaches to its measurement are considered, the calculation of
efficiency formula is suggested and justified. The issues of the application are stated.

Key words: statistical information actuality, sample error, efficiency of sampling observation.
